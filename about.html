About Jetseen

We're building the tools digital nomads need to travel the world without the compliance headaches.

Our Mission

For digital nomads, remote workers, and frequent travellers, keeping track of visa overstay rules and tax residency thresholds across multiple countries is a nightmare. One miscounted day can lead to fines, deportation, or unexpected tax liabilities.

Jetseen was built to solve this. We give you a clear, real-time dashboard that tracks your travel days against every rule that matters to you — so you can focus on living your life, not counting days on spreadsheets.

What We Believe

Privacy First

Your travel data is sensitive. It stays on your device, under your control. We don't store, track, or sell your information.

Simplicity

Complex rules shouldn't mean a complex interface. We distil complicated visa and tax logic into clear, actionable status indicators.

Accuracy

Our calculation engine is built to handle the nuances — rolling windows, weighted averages, tie-breaker tests, and country-specific tax years.

Accessibility

Travel compliance tools shouldn't cost a fortune. Jetseen is free to use and built for everyone, from first-time nomads to seasoned globetrotters.

Built for Nomads, by Nomads

Jetseen was born out of personal frustration. After one too many late-night spreadsheet sessions trying to figure out Schengen day counts and UK statutory residence tests, we decided to build the tool we wished existed.

Today, Jetseen supports 15+ country-specific rules including the Schengen 90/180 rule, US Substantial Presence Test, UK SRT, and many more — with new rules being added regularly.
